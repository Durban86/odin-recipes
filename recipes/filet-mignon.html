Filet Mignon

The Cadillac of Cuts!

The most tender piece of meat you'll ever put in your mouth. This delicacy goes for upwards of $100 in steakhouses around the world, with this recipe, you'll never want to have it anywhere else!

Ingredients

  • 6 oz filet mignon
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Thyme

The Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F
  2. Season steak with salt and pepper, all sides
  3. Heat oil in an oven safe pan until ripping hot and add steak
  4. Sear steak, approx 2-3 mins per side
  5. Once browned remove from heat and add your butter and thyme to the pan and baste steak with melted butter for 30 seconds
  6. Place pan in oven and let cook 3-5 minutes to desired liking
  7. Once done, remove pan and place steak on a plate, tent with tinfoil and let rest 5-10 minutes
  8. Top with left over butter and serve
