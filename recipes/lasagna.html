Lasagna

An Italian Classic!

This dish is sure to wow everyone at the dinner table. An italian classic made up of meat, cheese, and pasta constructed in such a way that your taste buds will be in sensory overload!

Ingredients

The Sauce

  • 2 tsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 3/4 cup diced onion
  • 3/4 cup diced bell pepper
  • 2 cloves minced garlic
  • 28 oz can tomato sauce
  • 3 oz tomato paste
  • 14 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Salt to taste

The Assembly

  • 9 dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta
  • 24 oz grated mozzarella
  • 4 oz grated parmesan

The Steps

  1. Put water on to boil, make sure it's salted
  2. On medium high heat add your oil to the sauce pot and add the ground beef, cook until brown. Remove the beef and set aside leaving some fat behind
  3. Add the bell pepper and onion to the sauce pot and saute until soft about 4-5 minutes, then add your garlic and cook another 30 second. Add your beef back to the pan, reduce heat to medium low and cook another 5 minutes
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste
  5. Season your mixture with oregano, parsley, italian seasoning, garlic powder, and salt.
  6. Sprinkle in red wine vinegar and sugar
  7. Bring sauce to a low simmer and let cook for 45 minutes, stirring often
  8. Boil lasagna noodles al dente per package directions
  9. Preheat oven to 375 F
  10. In a casserole dish, spoon a layer of sauce. Add 3 lasagna noodles, add another layer of sauce, add a layer of ricotta and mozzarella, add another layer of 3 lasagna noodles, add another layer of ricotta and mozzarella, add the final 3 lasagna noodles, cover with the rest of the sauce and top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan
  11. Bake for 45 minutes, uncover for the final 10 minutes
  12. Let rest 15 minutes and serve
